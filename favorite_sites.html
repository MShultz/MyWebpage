These are my favorite Websites

Here we go!

When not spending my time working on homework, there are a few websites that I spend my time on. For the most part, my favorite websites do not take up the majority of my time. I hope that you'll find the same enjoyments out of them as I do. These pair quite well with my interests, and, once you traverse that page (if you already haven't), I'm sure you'll see the correlation! Take some time enjoying everything; I'll be here when you get back!

  1. RpNation ~ A writing website
  2. SCP ~ A reading website
  3. Youtube ~ A video website
  4. Facebook ~ A social media website
  5. Tumblr ~ A 'I have nothing else to waste my life on' website

A little bit about my choices

RpNation

RpNation is a community where one can go and write as they desire. They can co-write with other community members or they can choose to write short stories or books alone.

SCP Foundation

Secure, Contain, Protect Foundation (SCP) is a website that contains the articles of different creatures contained and documents by the SCP. While fictional, the stories highlight a hint of horror and supernatural that I really enjoy. Some of my favorite articles are SCP-087 and SCP-072.

Youtube

Facebook and Tumblr

When I have nothing to do, these are the websites that let me scroll until I can't scroll anymore. They're pretty much time wasters, but since they help me keep up with family and friends, they aren't too bad.
